Built from a tweet: scrolls like social media, posts are your Kindle highlights

Year 2026
Role Solo Developer
Next.jsReactSCSSpdf.js
Repository Demonstration

The Problem

Someone tweeted "Can someone build this app?" wanting a way to revisit
their Kindle highlights in a more engaging format.

The Solution

A Twitter-like feed for your Kindle book highlights. Import, browse, and
save your favorite quotes.

Key Features:

  • Import highlights from Kindle's My Clippings.txt or Amazon PDF exports
  • X.com-style dark theme optimized for reading
  • Heart and save favorite passages
  • Privacy-first: all data stored locally in browser (no account required)
  • Responsive design for desktop, tablet, and mobile
  • Built quickly in response to a Twitter request
